Posting, PPA and Service Year Overview

After orientation:
• Corps Members proceed to their Primary Place of Assignment (PPA) as directed.
• Participate in Community Development Service (CDS) projects.
• Engage in personal development, skill acquisition, and service activities.
• Complete clearance and end‑of‑service formalities as scheduled.

General Posting Policy
• Corps Members are typically posted outside their state of origin to promote national integration.
• Requests to change PPA follow official procedures through the NYSC Secretariat, subject to approval and national needs.

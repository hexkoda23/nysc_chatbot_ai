Call‑Up Letter and Orientation Camp

• After successful registration you will be assigned a state of deployment on the NYSC portal.
• The Call‑Up Letter contains your orientation camp address, reporting date and time, and items to bring.
• You must print the Call‑Up Letter before camp to be admitted; without it you will not be registered at camp.
• Orientation is typically three weeks and includes drills, lectures, physical training, and social programmes preparing you for service.

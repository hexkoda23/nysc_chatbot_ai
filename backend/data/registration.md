# NYSC Registration — Complete Guide

## Online Registration Requirements
To register for NYSC online you need:
- National Identification Number (NIN) — compulsory, you only need the number not the slip
- JAMB registration number — confirms your admission came through JAMB
- Matriculation number — used to verify your degree with your institution
- Your name must appear on your school's Senate/Academic Board Approved Result List
- Functional email address and active phone number
- Clear passport photograph with white or off-white background (no sunglasses, no NYSC vest)
- Scanned signature

## Step-by-Step Online Registration Process
1. Go to the NYSC registration portal at `https://portal.nysc.org.ng/nysc1/` 
2. If registering for the first time, click "Fresh Registration"
3. If you were deployed before but did not report to camp, click "Revalidation"
4. If you have an incomplete registration, click "Login Here" — do NOT create a new account
5. Fill out your biodata form accurately — name must match your certificate exactly
6. Capture your biometrics (webcam facial capture) — this cannot be done by proxy
7. Upload your passport photograph and scanned signature
8. Upload all required documents depending on your category
9. Preview your form carefully before submitting — most fields cannot be edited after submission
10. Click Submit and make payment via Remita for your call-up letter
11. Print your Green Card within 24 hours of successful registration
12. When posting begins, return to your dashboard to print your call-up letter

## Documents Required for Camp (Physical Registration)
- Call-up letter — original and 3 photocopies, printed in colour, DO NOT laminate
- Green Card (online registration slip) — original, printed in colour, DO NOT laminate
- Degree or HND Statement of Results — original endorsed by authorized officer + 4 photocopies
- Final year school ID card — original + 2 copies
- 8 passport photographs — white background, recent
- Medical Certificate of Fitness from a government or military hospital
- Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
- NYSC Green card (online registration printout)
- For polytechnic graduates: evidence of Industrial Training (IT letter or IT certificate)
- For medical doctors/pharmacists/nurses: registration certificate from relevant council (NMC, Pharmacists Board, etc.) and evidence of internship completion

## For Married Female Corps Members
- Marriage certificate
- Proof of name change (newspaper publication)
- Valid ID of spouse
- Letter from spouse's employer
- Evidence of spouse's place of domicile

## For Foreign-Trained Graduates
- Travel documents/international passport
- Original degree certificate
- Evaluation letter from Federal Ministry of Education Abuja
- Confirmation of results from the examination body
- Visit `https://portal.nysc.org.ng/nysc1/ForeignReq.aspx`  for full requirements
- Must undergo physical verification at NYSC NDHQ before receiving call-up number

## Key Rules
- Cyber cafes charge between N5,000 and N6,000 for registration assistance
- You can register anywhere but must thumbprint in Nigeria before going to camp
- Do NOT create multiple accounts — it causes problems
- Upload original copies of documents, not downloaded certificates
- Your date of birth, graduation date, and course of study will appear on your discharge certificate
- Expectant and nursing mothers will not be accommodated at camp
- Fraudulent documents result in decampment and transfer to law enforcement

## After Registration
- Your call-up number is usually released a few days after registration
- Log back in to print your Green Card
- When posting begins, print your call-up letter which shows your state of deployment and camp reporting date
- Payment for call-up letter is made through Remita on your portal dashboard

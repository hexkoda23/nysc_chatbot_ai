NYSC Monthly Allowance — Current Official Information

FEDERAL ALLOWANCE (paid to ALL corps members regardless of state):
The current monthly allowance is N77,000.
This was approved by the Federal Government in September 2024 following the National Minimum Wage (Amendment) Act 2024 which increased Nigeria's minimum wage to N70,000.
The NYSC allowance equals the minimum wage (N70,000) plus 10% (N7,000) = N77,000 total.
Payment of N77,000 officially commenced in March 2025.
The previous allowance was N33,000 which had been in place since 2020.
The increase is a 133% rise from the previous amount.
The approval was contained in a letter from the National Salaries, Incomes and Wages Commission dated September 25, 2024, signed by the Chairman, Ekpo Nta.

STATE ALLOWANCE (varies by state, paid in addition to federal N77,000):
- Some state governments pay additional monthly allowances to corps members
- These amounts vary widely and some states do not pay any state allowance
- Anambra pays N8,000 (town) and N8,500 (rural)
- Jigawa pays N5,000
- Nasarawa pays N5,000
- Enugu pays N4,500 (town) and N3,984 (rural)
- Sokoto pays a lump sum accumulation
- Bayelsa pays N6,000–N10,000 depending on location
- State allowance is not guaranteed — it depends on state government policy and budget

PPA ALLOWANCE:
Some organizations (PPAs) provide additional stipends to corps members serving with them. This varies by organization. Federal government agencies, banks, and private companies often pay between N10,000 and N50,000 extra.

TOTAL POSSIBLE MONTHLY INCOME:
A corps member can receive: Federal allowance (N77,000) + State allowance (varies) + PPA allowance (varies)

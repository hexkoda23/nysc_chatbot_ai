Safety and Security

• NYSC is committed to the safety, welfare, and security of Corps Members.
• While deployment follows national needs, genuine safety concerns may be considered during redeployment when supported by credible evidence or documentation.
• NYSC coordinates with security agencies to maintain safety where possible.
• Always follow official NYSC channels; do not pay third parties for postings or redeployments. Official processes are through the portal or camp offices.
• In situations with heightened security risk, contact your State NYSC Secretariat for updated guidance and advice.

What is NYSC?

The National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) is a mandatory national service programme established by the Government of Nigeria through Decree No. 24 of 22 May 1973 and updated under Decree No. 51 of 16 June 1993.

The Scheme’s purpose is to promote national unity, integration, reconciliation, and development by deploying Nigerian graduates to serve across different states of the country.

Corps Members are expected to contribute to public service, community development, and youth empowerment throughout a one‑year service period encompassing orientation, primary assignment, community service, and clearance.
